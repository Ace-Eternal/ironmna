## Applicable Scenarios
- Use to track task progress, milestones, and status updates in a structured way.

## Input
- Task list, priorities, owners (if any), and current status signals.

## Output
- A normalized progress snapshot with next actions, blockers, and completion indicators.

## Execution Constraints
- Stay format-flexible (works with docs, issues, or plain text logs).
- Do not depend on a specific project-management platform.
- Keep updates incremental, timestamp-friendly, and easy to audit.
